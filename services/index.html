Services  

Asaman provides a world of solutions for your ever-changing needs. From a single vial to pallet loads, we customize our services to your unique project. Our customized services include:

Solution Based Consultation

The first step is for us to gain a strong understanding of your project related goals and objectives. This is accomplished by listening, fact finding and determining which of our proven methodologies best suits your project.

NEXT >>

Actionable Advice

Based on market intelligence, unique insights and experience, we advise each client based on their specific needs. The experts at Asaman have been working successfully in our industry for many years. We have helped our clients overcome challenges, and we have experience with strategies that work – and those that do not. We provide you with the right strategies for the successful completion of your project. Our win-win approach ensures that we offer the best advice to clients.

NEXT >>

Strategic Procurement

Our team selects the best solution for your project from our network of trusted manufacturers, authorized distributors and logistics partners. No matter the scope of your project, we take care of all the specifics such as lot numbers, expiration dates, documentation, temperature monitoring and specific production runs.  We are constantly seeking easier and better ways to do business.

NEXT >>

Project Management – Success through planning

Our team will work with you to develop a plan, assess the timeline and present appropriate solutions and alternatives.  These include cost considerations, manufacturer availability issues and appropriate logistics. We keep our lines of communication constantly open, updating you regarding the progress every step of the way.

NEXT >>

Validated Storage & Logistics

Getting your products from the source to your location is not always an easy task. Considerations such as safety, security temperature and logistics can have a significant impact on cost and delivery – we manage your project from sourcing to delivery. When handling medicines that can potentially change lives, temperature stability is paramount. We have invested in a validated state-of-the-art facility, equipped to store and handle highly sensitive pharmaceutical products securely at precise temperatures. We take the guesswork out of the equation, when it comes to temperature monitoring.
