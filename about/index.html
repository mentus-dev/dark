About Us  

Welcome to Asaman

Asaman is a fully licensed specialty pharmaceutical distributor, assisting a wide range of clients with their clinical trial needs.

We are not your average pharmaceutical distributor.

Asaman specializes in pharmaceutical procurement of comparator drugs, often those necessary for the most complex clinical trials. We are dedicated to making the clinical trial process simpler, by distributing high quality comparator drugs in a timely, precise and cost efficient manner.

Our experience and proven track record has given us the privilege and responsibility to serve the needs of a wide client base. We work closely with each of our clients to provide streamlined, accurate and customized services.

We believe that our dedication, time tested procurement process and expert knowledge are extremely effective in helping our clients achieve their business objectives.
