Quality  

Pharmaceutical Procurement

When you choose Asaman, you have the benefit of knowing we have all the necessary processes in place to ensure your products have been procured ethically and from approved sources. We take quality every bit as seriously as you do.

Now more than ever, regulations regarding safety and product integrity require that medicinal products are stored and maintained within the designated temperature ranges. Patient safety is paramount, we ensure that your products are received and stored in optimum storage conditions.

In fact, at Asaman we have proactively been ahead of the curve. Our state-of-the-art facility meets and exceeds industry standards. Our system allows for up-to-the minute data collection, which we share with our clients. We work with approved logistics service providers, to ensure that products are delivered in pristine condition.

Enjoy peace of mind, knowing that we mitigate risks and ensure that products we source are transported and stored to the exact manufacturer’s specification.
